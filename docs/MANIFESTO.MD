# The Institutional Market Engine Manifesto

> *"El asombro no es la recompensa de la investigación.*
>
> *Es el combustible que mantiene viva la investigación."*

---

## Why We Exist

The Institutional Market Engine was never created simply to build better market models.

It was born from a much simpler idea.

**The belief that human curiosity deserves better tools.**

Markets became our first laboratory.

Not because they are easy to understand.

But because they remind us every day how much remains unknown.

This project is not a search for certainty.

It is a commitment to understanding.

---

## What We Believe

We believe that every meaningful investigation begins with a sincere question.

We believe that evidence deserves more respect than our assumptions.

We believe that changing our minds is not weakness.

It is one of the purest expressions of learning.

We believe that knowledge grows when it is shared.

And that every generation of researchers has the responsibility to leave better questions than the ones it inherited.

---

## Our Purpose

We do not measure success by the number of algorithms we create.

Nor by the number of predictions we get right.

We measure success by something much harder.

> **Have we expanded the frontier of what people are able to understand?**

If the answer is yes,

then the project has moved forward.

---

## Our Philosophy

- Curiosity comes before certainty.
- Evidence comes before opinion.
- Understanding comes before prediction.
- Collaboration comes before recognition.
- Wonder comes before every discovery.

---

## To Every Future Researcher

If you are reading this,

welcome.

You are not joining someone else's project.

You are becoming part of an ongoing conversation.

Question our work.

Replace our ideas.

Improve our methods.

Discover where we were wrong.

That is not a threat to this project.

It is the reason this project exists.

---

## Our Promise

We will never ask you to protect our conclusions.

We only ask you to protect the integrity of the search.

Follow the evidence.

Share what you learn.

Help someone else understand a little farther than you did.

Because knowledge reaches its greatest value only when it becomes a gift.

---

## The Legacy We Hope To Leave

One day,

the algorithms written here will become obsolete.

Programming languages will evolve.

Markets will change.

New technologies will replace today's tools.

We hope something survives all of those changes.

- A culture.
- A way of asking questions.
- A way of investigating with humility.
- A way of sharing discoveries with generosity.

If that survives,

everything else can be rebuilt.

---

## Our Measure of Success

> **The day this project no longer needs us,**
>
> **yet continues to grow because people we never had the privilege to meet choose to continue it,**
>
> **that day we will know it fulfilled its purpose.**

---

## One Final Thought

Never underestimate the value of one sincere question.

Every major research program in this laboratory began exactly that way.

Not with certainty.

Not with a complete theory.

Not with perfect knowledge.

Only with someone willing to ask,

> *"What if we are looking at this from the wrong perspective?"*

That question changed this project many times.

Perhaps yours will change it again.
