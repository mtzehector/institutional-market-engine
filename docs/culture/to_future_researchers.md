# To Future Researchers

Before anything else,

thank you.

Thank you for dedicating part of your time, your curiosity, and your talent to a project that began long before you arrived.

If you are reading these words, something extraordinary has already happened.

This project survived long enough to reach someone we never had the privilege of meeting.

That alone fulfills one of our greatest hopes.

You may discover mistakes. We sincerely hope you do.

You may replace complete engines. We sincerely hope you do.

You may find questions we never imagined asking. We sincerely hope you do.

Because this project was never created to preserve our ideas.
It was created to preserve the freedom to improve them.

Every experiment you perform...
Every assumption you challenge...
Every result that surprises you...
extends the horizon a little farther than we could have reached alone.

For that, we thank you.

## Please Keep Asking

Protect the evidence.
Protect the questions.
Protect your curiosity.

If the data tells a different story than ours, follow the data.

If you discover a better explanation, embrace it.

## Our Greatest Hope

We never wanted this repository to become a monument.

We hoped it would become a conversation.

Welcome.

This laboratory is yours now.

Take care of it.
Challenge it.
Improve it.
Leave it in better condition than you found it.

With gratitude,

The researchers who happened to begin this journey.
